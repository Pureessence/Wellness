Our Blessed Ingredients

Our products are built upon the benefits of ingredients revered across cultures for centuries, recognized for their healing and nourishing properties, and validated by modern science.

  • Black Seed (Nigella Sativa / Habbatus Sauda): Known for its potent health benefits.
  • Honey (especially Sidr Honey): Revered for its healing properties.
  • Olive Oil (Zaitoon): Valued for its health and nutritional benefits.
  • Dates (especially Ajwa Dates): Known for their energy and nutritional value.
  • Barley (for Talbina): A traditional and nourishing grain.
  • Figs (Teen): Appreciated for their health benefits.
  • Grapes/Raisins (A'nab): Rich in antioxidants and natural energy.
